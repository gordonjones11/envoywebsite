The Challenge

Operating a modern pulp and paper mill isn't easy.

We hear it every day. Four problems come up again and again — and Envoy is built to help with all of them.

Unplanned Downtime

Unexpected equipment failures that cut into output and profit — often with little warning and no clear path to root cause.

Process Instability

Machine breaks and process fluctuations that make consistent production difficult — especially across shifts and grade changes.

Cost Pressure

Energy, chemical, and maintenance costs keep rising while margins keep shrinking — leaving little room for inefficiency.

Lean Teams, High Expectations

Smaller ops teams handling more responsibilities — with fewer process experts to lean on as senior talent retires.
