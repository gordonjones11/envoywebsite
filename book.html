The Intelligent Factory:   The Book

Leading the Intelligent Factory

Seven Disciplines for Applying AI in Brownfield Process Manufacturing — a practical leadership guide for operations leaders in continuous process industries who want to apply AI without rebuilding their factories.

← Intelligent Factory: Start Here

The Central Thesis

The factory of the future won't be built new.

It will be defined by how well leaders connect, contextualize, govern, and improve the assets they already have. Most mills can't afford a greenfield transformation — and they don't need one. Leadership, not equipment, is the differentiator.

Inside the Book

Three parts. Seven disciplines.

An executive-friendly read (~15,000 words) with diagrams throughout — built as a repeatable framework, not a technology survey.

Part I — Reframing the Leadership Problem

Economics Before Algorithms and Context Beats Connectivity — why transformation starts with operational value and operational context, not tools.

Part II — Building Intelligence on Legacy Assets

Retrofit the Information Layer, Put AI in the Operator's Workflow, and Govern AI Like a Safety-Critical System — making a brownfield mill intelligent without replacing it.

Part III — Scaling the Intelligent Factory

Escape the Pilot Trap and Build the Adaptive Mill — turning wins into systems and arriving at a factory that continuously learns.

Plus: Supporting Chapters

The Brownfield Truth, The Factory of 2035 — a vision narrative where people remain central — and The Ten Myths of AI in Manufacturing.

The Audience

Written for the people who run mills.

Mill managers, operations managers, manufacturing executives, process engineers, reliability and maintenance leaders, and digital transformation leaders — anyone accountable for making an existing operation perform better.

Status

The book is being written.

Publication details, chapter previews, and how to get a copy will appear here. In the meantime, the framework is taking shape across this section — start with the Seven Disciplines.
